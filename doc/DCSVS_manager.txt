ABOUT THIS DCSVS MANAGER        *DCSVS_manager_about*

This is a really simple manager for DCSVS plugins.

==============================================================================

USAGE                           *DCSVS_manager_usage*

To activate plugin, add below to your .vimrc
>
        let g:DCSVS_load_<plugin_name> = 1

For example, to activate betterdigraphs_utf8
>
        let g:DCSVS_load_betterdigraphs_utf8 = 1
